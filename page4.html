When choosing a CCTV camera, consider the following factors:

~Resolution: Higher resolution cameras provide clearer images.
~Field of View: Determine the area you want to cover to choose the right lens.
~Night Vision: If you need surveillance in low-light conditions, opt for cameras with infrared capabilities.
~Indoor/Outdoor Use: Ensure the camera is weatherproof for outdoor use.
~Connectivity: Decide between wired or wireless based on your property layout and installation preference.

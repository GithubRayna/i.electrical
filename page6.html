Licensed Electrician

The licensed electrician will also need to be able to determine service needs for the cases assigned to them. The licensed electrician will be responsible for the installation and repair of electrical works including but not limited to, electrical points, switches, power points, isolators, and DB boxes. Licensed electricians must have experience working and doing electrical works in Singapore HDB / Condo / Landed and commercial areas.

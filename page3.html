How to install Normal light fitting

One of the most vibrant and visible pieces of decor in your home. But where do you even start? Don't worry, we have a few easy steps to get you going as you install your stunning statement piece.
1.Safety First!
The first consideration is Safety. Make sure to shut off power  to the fixture.Then stall by uninstalling the old fixture and confirm the electrical box is placed where you would like.
2.Ceiling of Strength
Verify that the beam or concrete structure (where you intend to hang your lighting fixture) can support the weight of your light. 
3.Install like a Pro
Unpack your light and assemble your light. First, attach the mounting bracket onto the electrical box. Now, it’s time to wire the fixture. Each lighting fixture comes with a ground wire, positive wire and a negative. Install the ground wires first.
4.Finish Strong
If your light has additional elements, now is the time to install or assemble them. Add your light bulbs and, with the flick of a switch, you’ve got light!
